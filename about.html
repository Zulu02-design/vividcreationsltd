About Vivid Creations Ltd

EVERY STITCH
TELLS A STORY

Rooted In Craft, Made For Today

Vivid Creations Ltd began with a simple belief: that clothing deserves care, not just construction. What started as a small tailoring service has grown into a home for anyone who wants their clothes altered, repaired, repurposed, or reimagined rather than replaced.

Based in Solwezi, Zambia, we work with our hands and with heart — blending classic tailoring skill with a love for African print and everyday design, so every piece that leaves our workshop carries a little more character than it arrived with.

Our Mission

To preserve the art of tailoring while giving it a modern edge — helping every customer extend the life of what they own, express themselves through African print, and wear clothes made with real skill and intention.

What We Do

Clothing Alterations

Precise fitting and adjustments so every piece sits exactly the way it should.

Repairs

From torn seams to worn hems, we mend clothes so you can keep wearing what you love.

Repurposing

Old or outgrown clothes reworked into something new — good for your wardrobe and the planet.

Cushion Covers

Custom-made covers, tailored to your space, in fabric and prints that suit your home.

Chitenge Handcrafts

Handmade pieces crafted from African print fabric, celebrating color, pattern, and heritage.

Sun Hats

Handcrafted sun hats built for Zambian sun — practical, stylish, and made to last.
